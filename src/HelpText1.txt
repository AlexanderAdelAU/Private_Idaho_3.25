
Compose the message in the Message text box.

In the To: text box, enter the PGP user ID of person you'll be sending the
message to.  This is usually an e-mail address.  You must have already added
the person's key to your public key ring.  You can also click on the To box to
access your or your PGP Key ring.From the PGP menu, choose the 'Encrypt message' command.
